Hey!
I am a second-year Trinity student and Huang Fellow at Duke University from Martinsville, NJ. I am planning to double major in Computer Science and Neuroscience. Academically, the human brain has been the center of my fascination with biology for years. The idea that chemicals, proteins, cells, and ciruits can construct a complex organ with the capability of sensing, understanding, and acting on the world is mind-blowing to me. Concurrently, I grew interested in the computational analysis involved in neuroscience research. Ever since, computer science and engineering has been the focus of my academic studies.

Outside the classroom, I am interested in the environment, philosophy, and photography. I am also a badminton player with over a decade of international competitive experience. Feel free to contact me with any questions or comments, and I hope you enjoyed getting to know me a little better!
